Contact Drawer Test

Testing: Click the "CONTACT" button in the header to open the integrated contact drawer.

The drawer should:
  • Slide in from the right
  • Show a gold background
  • Display the contact form
  • Close when clicking the overlay, close button, or pressing Escape

Test Instructions

1. Look at the header at the top of the page
2. Click the "CONTACT" button (desktop) or open mobile menu and click "CONTACT" (mobile)
3. The contact drawer should slide in from the right
4. Try closing it using:

  • The "CLOSE" button
  • Clicking the dark overlay
  • Pressing the Escape key

Form Fields

The drawer includes these form fields:

  • NAME
  • PHONE
  • EMAIL
  • TEAM
  • SUBJECT
  • MESSAGE

Console Logging

Open your browser's developer console (F12) to see action events logged when you click the CONTACT button.
